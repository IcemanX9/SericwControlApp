Policy Information

Internal Rats and Mice Control

Only glue boards or snap traps or nontoxic bait to be used as per the chemical list.

Unlock boxes with key provided.

Check all boxes for any sign of infestation. Example: Rodent droppings or trapped rodents.

Clean inside and outside of box using a brush only, no soap to be used.

Remove old glue board and insert new board or check traps and reset if applicable.

Remove old nontoxic bait as per chemical list and insert fresh nontoxic bait into pins on the inside of box if applicable.

Lock box, sign and date and record infestation on wall bait station marker.

All services will be recorded in the pest control file under the applicable sections.
